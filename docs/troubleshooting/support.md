For account and support questions, email our team at [mygenassist@bayer.com](mailto:mygenassist@bayer.com).

myGenAssist Studio is provided for Bayer internal users. For documentation, visit https://docs.int.bayer.com/baychatgpt.

## Getting Started

myGenAssist Studio connects to the myGenAssist backend using your Bayer Azure AD credentials. Simply:

1. Launch the application
2. Sign in with your Bayer credentials
3. Start working with your AI workforce

## File Storage

Your task files are stored locally on your machine:

- **Windows:** `C:\Users\[YourUsername]\mygenassist-studio\[YourEmailPrefix]\task_[TaskID]`
- **Mac:** `/Users/[YourUsername]/mygenassist-studio/[YourEmailPrefix]/task_[TaskID]`

## FAQ

### How do I update myGenAssist Studio?

Download the latest version from your internal distribution channel and install it over your existing installation.

### Can I use custom models?

myGenAssist Studio connects to the myGenAssist backend. Contact the team at mygenassist@bayer.com for information about available models.

### How do I report a bug?

Use the Bug Report button in the application or email mygenassist@bayer.com with a description of the issue.
